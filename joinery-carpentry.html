Our Comprehensive Joinery & Carpentry Services

At Morris Property Firm (MPF), we offer expert joinery and carpentry services designed to enhance the beauty, functionality, and value of your property. Whether you're looking to add custom woodwork, install bespoke cabinetry, or require high-quality carpentry solutions, our skilled craftsmen are equipped to deliver outstanding results.

Serving Bangor and North Wales, MPF is committed to providing personalized and reliable carpentry services that cater to your specific needs and preferences. We use premium materials and innovative techniques to create woodwork that is both aesthetically pleasing and durable.

  • Custom Woodwork: Tailored woodwork solutions to complement your home's interior design and architecture.
  • Bespoke Cabinetry: Handcrafted cabinets designed to maximize storage space and enhance your living areas.
  • Flooring Installation: Expert installation of hardwood, laminate, and engineered wood flooring for a seamless finish.
  • Staircase Construction: Beautiful and sturdy staircase designs that serve as focal points in your home.
  • Window and Door Frames: High-quality wood frames that provide both functionality and aesthetic appeal.
  • Outdoor Carpentry: Custom outdoor structures such as decks, pergolas, and garden sheds to enhance your outdoor living spaces.
