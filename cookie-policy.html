Cookie Policy

Understand how MPF uses cookies and how you can manage them

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help that site remember your actions and preferences (login, language, font size, and other display settings) over a period of time so you don’t have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

2. How We Use Cookies

Morris Property Firm uses cookies to:

  • Ensure site functionality – keep navigation menus, forms, and cookie banners working properly.
  • Remember preferences – such as your cookie-consent choices, so we don’t ask you every time.
  • Analyse performance – via Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site and improve the experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Set

Category Cookie Name / Provider Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary cookieConsent (MPF) Stores your banner choice (accept / decline). 6 months
Analytics _ga, _gid (Google Analytics) Counts visits, tracks pages viewed, helps us spot problems. Up to 2 years

4. Third-Party Cookies

We embed content and scripts from trusted partners (e.g. Google Analytics, Bootstrap CDN). These providers may set their own cookies. We have no control over the settings of these cookies – please check the third-party websites for more information.

5. Managing & Disabling Cookies

You have several options:

  • Use our on-site cookie banner to accept or decline optional cookies.
  • Adjust your browser settings to refuse or delete cookies. Note that some site functionality may break if you disable essential cookies.
  • Install Google’s Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted on this page and sign-posted via the cookie banner.
