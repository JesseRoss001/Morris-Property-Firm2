About Morris Property Firm

Your Trusted Partner in Property Restoration, Repairs, and Maintenance

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Robin Morris, Morris Property Firm (MPF) was established to address the growing need for reliable property restoration, repairs, and maintenance services in North Wales. With a strong passion for maintaining and enhancing properties, Robin aimed to create a company that homeowners and landlords could trust to care for their valuable investments.

Over the years, MPF has built a reputation for excellence, delivering high-quality services that meet and exceed client expectations. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for property services in Bangor and the surrounding areas.

Learn more about our [Painting Services](painting.html), [Windows and Doors](windows-doors.html), [Ground Work](ground-work.html), and [Builder Services](builder.html) to see how we can help you enhance your property.

Meet the Founder

Robin Morris, the owner of Morris Property Firm, has been in the construction industry since leaving school. Over the years, he has successfully managed several businesses, including one with a turnover of £1.5 million. With a deep understanding of construction and property management, Robin founded MPF to provide top-tier restoration and maintenance services. His dedication to quality and customer satisfaction drives the company’s success.

Robin's expertise spans various aspects of property services, ensuring that MPF delivers comprehensive solutions tailored to each client's unique needs. His leadership fosters a culture of excellence, where every team member is committed to upholding the firm's high standards.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional property restoration, repairs, and maintenance services that exceed our clients' expectations. We strive to maintain the highest standards of quality, reliability, and professionalism in every project we undertake.

Whether it's a minor repair or a major renovation, MPF is dedicated to providing solutions that enhance the value and functionality of your property.
