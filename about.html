About Morris Property Firm

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Robin Morris, Morris Property Firm was established to address the growing need for reliable property restoration, repairs, and maintenance services in North Wales. With a strong passion for maintaining and enhancing properties, Robin aimed to create a company that homeowners and landlords could trust to care for their valuable investments.

Meet the Founder

Robin Morris, the owner of Morris Property Firm, has been in the construction industry since leaving school. Over the years, he has successfully managed several businesses, including one with a turnover of £1.5 million. With a deep understanding of construction and property management, Robin founded Morris Property Firm to provide top-tier restoration and maintenance services. His dedication to quality and customer satisfaction drives the company’s success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional property restoration, repairs, and maintenance services that exceed our clients' expectations. We strive to maintain the highest standards of quality, reliability, and professionalism in every project we undertake.
