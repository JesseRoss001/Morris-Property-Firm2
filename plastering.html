Our Plastering & Skimming Services

At Morris Property Firm (MPF), we specialize in delivering high-quality plastering and skimming solutions. From repairing damaged walls to skimming entire properties, our team ensures seamless finishes that stand the test of time.

Based in Bangor and serving North Wales, our reputation is built on professionalism, meticulous attention to detail, and client satisfaction—every project is handled with the utmost care.

  • Interior Plastering: Smooth finishes for walls, ceilings, and partitions.
  • Skimming: Transform rough or uneven surfaces into sleek, paint-ready walls.
  • Patch Repairs: Quick fixes for cracks, dents, and damaged plaster areas.
  • Float & Set: Traditional plastering method for the ultimate durable finish.
  • Plasterboard Installation: Professional fitting of drywall before skimming.
  • Surface Preparation: Cleaning, sealing, and priming for a flawless final result.
