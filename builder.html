Our Comprehensive Builder Services

At Morris Property Firm (MPF), we offer a full spectrum of builder services designed to meet all your construction needs. Whether you're planning a new build, a renovation, or an extension, our skilled team delivers exceptional craftsmanship and unparalleled service to bring your vision to life.

Serving Bangor and North Wales, MPF is committed to using high-quality materials and adhering to the highest standards of construction. We collaborate closely with our clients to ensure every project is executed flawlessly, on time, and within budget.

  • New Builds: Expert construction of new residential and commercial properties from the ground up.
  • Renovations: Comprehensive renovation services to update and enhance your existing spaces.
  • Extensions: Seamless building extensions that blend perfectly with your current structure.
  • Bespoke Projects: Custom construction solutions tailored to your specific requirements and design preferences.
  • Project Management: End-to-end project management ensuring smooth execution and timely completion.
  • Commercial Construction: Specialized services for constructing and renovating commercial properties, offices, and retail spaces.

Whether it's creating your dream home, expanding your business premises, or executing a unique construction project, MPF has the expertise and resources to deliver outstanding results. Trust our dedicated team to handle all aspects of your building project with professionalism, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence.
