The duration of a flooring installation depends on several factors, including the type of flooring, the size of the area, and the condition of the existing floor. On average:
  • Carpet Installation: Typically completed within one day for an average-sized room.
  • Laminate Flooring: Usually takes one to two days per room, depending on complexity.
  • Vinyl Flooring: Can be installed within a day, including preparation.
  • Wood Flooring: May take two to three days per room to ensure precise installation and finishing.
  • LVT Flooring: Similar to vinyl flooring, generally completed within a day.
We provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation to ensure transparency and accommodate your schedule.

Proper maintenance varies depending on the type of flooring:
  • Carpet Flooring: Regular vacuuming, prompt stain removal, and professional deep cleaning every 12-18 months.
  • Laminate Flooring: Sweep or vacuum regularly, mop with a damp cloth using a laminate-safe cleaner, and avoid excessive moisture.
  • Vinyl Flooring: Sweep or vacuum to remove debris, mop with a mild detergent solution, and use mats in high-traffic areas.
  • Wood Flooring: Dust regularly, use a wood-friendly cleaner, place protective pads under furniture, and refinish as needed.
  • LVT Flooring: Sweep or vacuum regularly, mop with a damp cloth using a non-abrasive cleaner, and avoid harsh chemicals.
We provide detailed maintenance guidelines during installation to help you keep your floors looking pristine for years to come.
